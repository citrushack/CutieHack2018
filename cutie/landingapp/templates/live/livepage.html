Ongoing events

Cutest Cuties: Grab a cutie and make it cute! At the end of of the night, we'll announce which cutie really is the cutest cutie.

Game Room: Want a little break from the all of the hacking? Come up to WCH 202 to unwind and play some games!

Sponsor Room: Come talk to our sponsors! You can come network with industry professionals and grab some swag by coming to WCH 205/206!

Learn New Languages!

Want to learn a new skill? It's actually not hard to learn a new language once you already know one!

Python: Download Python, and use it to make a video game, perform statistical analysis, and automate the boring stuff.

We have a Python workshop at 12PM in WCH 138!

HTML & CSS: Want to create a cool website? HTML and CSS are used in conjunction with each other to create the visuals of the web.

Deploy a website on GitHub Pages to claim your corner of the Internet!

API's: The Backbone of Hackathon Projects

API's allow you to connect your application with other external applications. There's API's for anything and everything!

Want to grab the weather?
Generate shiba inu images?
Pull Fortnite stats?

With API's you can make your project robust in a short amount of time.

Here is a repository of open API's.

Tutorials to Start Simple Software Hacks

Have an awesome idea for a hack but don't know where to incorporate it? Fear not! There's plenty of ways for you to get your idea out there for the world to see!

Chrome Extension: Chrome Extensions can have a wide range of complexity and functionality. Extensions are built using a variety of involved components. You can get started here!

Reddit Bot: Build a bot that scans reddit posts and/or comments for specific criteria. Here is a tutorial that teaches you how to build a Reddit bot with basic Python.

iOS Application: Maybe your idea would be best on a mobile device? Using Swift and Xcode, you can make an iOS app for anything! Click here to get started.

Simple Website: Deploy a website on GitHub Pages to claim your corner of the Internet!

Want to make a more complex web application? Check out our Flask workshop at 12PM in WCH 141!
